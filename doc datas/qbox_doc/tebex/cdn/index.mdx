The Qbox CDN is a powerful content delivery network powered by Cloudflare. Deliver your static assets with lightning speed and reliability worldwide.

## Who is this for?

Anyone running a "phone" script that has the ability to take in-game pictures. Or similar scripts that need to upload images and the like to a permanent location.

## What do I get?

You'll get 20 | 60 | 120 GB of storage space, depending on your Ko-fi tier. With a complete dashboard to manage your files, you can easily upload, delete, and view your assets.
And at the time of writing, limited organizational support.
